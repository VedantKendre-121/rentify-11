About us

Welcome to Rentify

Rentify is a dynamic rental booking platform that connects people who want to rent household items with those willing to lend them. Whether you need furniture, appliances, tools, or other household essentials, Rentify makes it easy to access them without the need for permanent ownership.

Key Features:
1️⃣ Rent or List Items
2️⃣ Secure Transactions
3️⃣ Smart Search & Filters
4️⃣ Flexible Rental Durations
5️⃣ Convenient Pickup & Delivery
